Top 10 Most Common Car Problems and How to Fix Them

If you own a car, chances are you’ve experienced at least one annoying issue that disrupts your day — whether it’s an engine that shakes, a battery that won’t start, or weird noises coming from under the hood.

At Carithm, our AI-powered tool helps identify real mechanical issues from symptoms — giving repair suggestions, severity scores, and cost estimates. Before you jump into diagnostics, here are the top 10 car problems every driver should be familiar with.

1. Engine Misfires

Symptoms: Rough idling, shaking, loss of power, check engine light.

Fix: Replace spark plugs, ignition coils; clean fuel injectors.

2. Dead or Weak Battery

Symptoms: Slow start, dim lights, electrical issues.

Fix: Replace battery, clean terminals, check alternator.

3. Overheating Engine

Symptoms: Steam, high temperature gauge, coolant smells.

Fix: Top off coolant, check radiator/thermostat.

4. Brake Problems

Symptoms: Squealing, soft pedal, poor braking.

Fix: Replace pads, top brake fluid, bleed lines.

5. Transmission Issues

Symptoms: Gear slipping, delays, noises.

Fix: Check fluid, service transmission.

6. Check Engine Light

Fix: Use Carithm to scan codes and identify real causes like misfires or emissions issues.

7. Starter Motor Problems

Symptoms: Clicking sound, no crank.

Fix: Replace starter, test battery.

8. Faulty Alternator

Symptoms: Dimming lights, warning light, dying battery.

Fix: Replace alternator, tighten belt.

9. Exhaust Smoke

Symptoms: Blue/white/black smoke.

Fix: Check gaskets, coolant, fuel system.

10. Suspension Problems

Symptoms: Bumpy ride, uneven tire wear.

Fix: Replace shocks, align wheels.
