🔧 How Carithm Works

Carithm is a free AI-powered car diagnostic tool that identifies problems based on symptoms you describe. Whether your engine shakes, your car stalls after starting, or the battery won’t crank — Carithm uses smart logic and real automotive data to pinpoint likely causes.

It also supports OBD-II trouble codes like P0300 (random misfire), P0171 (lean condition), P0420 (catalyst efficiency below threshold), and more. You’ll get insights into symptoms, common causes, fixes, and repair cost estimates.

No signup needed — just enter your car’s issue and let the AI guide you through a diagnosis. Perfect for DIYers, students, mechanics, and car enthusiasts.

Try queries like: “Car cranks but won’t start,” “Smoke from exhaust,” “OBD code P0301,” or “Overheating at idle.”
